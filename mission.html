OUR MISSION STATEMENT

By the grace of God, The Pattern Of Good Works Ministry strives to:

  • Worship God Almighty in holiness and reverence
  • Preach the good news of the Messiah Jesus Christ and the Imminent Kingdom of God through evangelism, Bible classes, lectures and organized spiritual forums
  • Prepare believers for the coming of the Lord Jesus Christ
  • Work for the unity of all Christians
  • Inculcate and restore Christian values into homes and families to encourage peace, unity and stability through prayers, counseling and spiritual ministration
  • Organize charities and relief operations
  • Promote crime free and drug free environments
