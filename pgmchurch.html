ABOUT OUR CHURCH

Pattern Of Good Works Ministry (PGM) is a place of worship, prayer, unity and spiritual growth. We are committed to spreading the gospel of Christ, raising devoted believers and creating a welcoming atmosphere for everyone.

Through our services, outreaches and programs, lives are transformed and souls are strengthened in faith and love.

INTRODUCTION

Pattern Of Good Works Ministry (PGM) started as a very small parish precisely in December 2012

The parish over the years went through different developmental challenges such as membership drive, finances but through the help of God and support from members, the church grew steadily.

Today, to God be the glory for the unprecedented growth of the church and the miraculous works through the hands of His anointed servants.

AIM

Our aim is to worship and adore God only in truth and spirit.

OBJECTIVE

  • To establish a Pentecostal Church of Christ with a clear difference rooted in Christ.
  • To create and cultivate a healthy church environment through genuine love, unity and fellowship.
  • To commit ourselves to the expansion and growth of the church through evangelism.
