CHURCH DEPARTMENTS
AND UNITS

Pattern Of Good Works Ministry

GENERAL OVERSEER

  • The General Overseer serves as the spiritual father of the church
  • He is the highest authority and coordinates the affairs of the church
  • He gives members a sense of belonging
  • He encourages members during difficult times
  • He has in-depth knowledge about members
  • Performs pastorial duties
  • Serves as chairman of church committees

MINISTERS

  • Teach and preach the word of God truthfully
  • Receive revelations through divine consultation
  • Draw sinners back to God
  • Pray continually for the church
  • Foretell events through divine revelation
  • Carry out counseling and guidance

CHOIR

  • Ministers through music
  • Ignites the church with the Holy Spirit
  • Promotes unity through ministrations

COUNCIL OF ELDERS

  • Supports the pastor in charge
  • Prays for church members
  • Helps settle disputes
  • Supports the church with resources and experience
  • Serves as soul winners for Christ

ADMINISTRATIVE DEPARTMENT

  • Assists in planning and strategizing church growth
  • Manages records and church data bank
  • Ensures smooth exchange of files and documents
  • Collects and collates reports from departments

USHERING UNIT

  • Serves as ushers
  • Keeps the church sanctuary clean always
  • Cleans and sanctifies the sanctuary
  • Ensures orderliness in church

SANITATION UNIT

  • Keeps the church sanctuary clean always
  • Ensures protection house cleanliness
  • Maintains safe toilets

EVANGELISM AND WELFARE

  • Propagates the gospel through evangelism
  • Wins souls into the vineyard
  • Assists members in need
  • Plans spiritual growth strategies
  • Follows up members and their wellbeing

YOUTHS (18 YEARS AND ABOVE)

  • Prepares youths to be ambassadors of Christ
  • Brings new members into church
  • Supports innovation and progressive ideas
  • Prepares youths for leadership

TEENAGERS (13-17 YEARS OLD)

  • Gives biblical teachings to teenagers
  • Teaches church doctrines and conduct
  • Instills discipline and fear of God
  • Develops communication and moral skills

CHILDREN (5-12 YEARS OLD)

  • Introduces children to Jesus Christ
  • Builds strong Christian foundations
  • Encourages discipline and obedience
  • Teaches friendship and love
