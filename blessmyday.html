What is Bless My Day?

Bless My Day is one of the most special and beloved monthly events of Pattern Of Good Works Ministry. Held every third Saturday of the month, it is a powerful gathering designed to set the atmosphere for divine encounters, breakthroughs and spiritual refreshing.

The event is personally hosted by our founder, Pastor Tajudeen Hakeem, alongside invited ministers of God who bring the word, worship and intercession. Every session is unique, spirit-filled and leaves attendees transformed and strengthened in faith.

Whether you are believing God for a miracle, seeking spiritual direction or simply hungry for more of God, Bless My Day is the place to be every third Saturday of every Month.
