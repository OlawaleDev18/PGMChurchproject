Pastor Tajudeen Hakeem

Biography

Pastor Tajudeen Hakeem is the visionary founder of Pattern Of Good Works Ministry (PGM), a ministry dedicated to spreading the gospel, impacting lives and raising believers rooted in faith, truth and holiness.

Through years of dedication, spiritual leadership and evangelism, he has guided many individuals into deeper fellowship with God while building a ministry focused on love, prayer, worship and community transformation.

His teachings continue to inspire spiritual growth, unity and commitment to God's purpose. Under his leadership, the ministry has expanded through outreach programs, youth empowerment, prayer conferences and community development.

The vision of Pattern Of Good Works Ministry remains centered on raising a generation committed to righteous living and kingdom impact.
